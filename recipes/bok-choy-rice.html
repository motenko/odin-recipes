Bok Choy

Description

An ideal recipe for reducing waste in the kitchen, fried rice is a great way to use up spare tofu, leftover meats or wobbly vegetables. If it’s a bright, fresh slew of greens and herbs you’re after, this recipe supplies a high ratio of vegetables to rice.

Ingredients

  • Brown Rice
  • Bok Choy
  • Shallots
  • Olive Oil
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in skillet
  2. Add thin sliced shallots
  3. Heat rice in microwave
  4. Add sliced Bok Choy to pan
  5. After veggies cooked, add rice
  6. Fry rice with soy sauce
